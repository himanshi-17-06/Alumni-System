It will be a web-based application that can act as an interactive medium between the old student (alumni) and our college (ZHCET). It will help the college to track old students and to track them for future endeavors. One of the major objectives of this system will be to keep persistent records of all college graduates..

Alumni are the people who represent the Institution in the real world. Alumni website is created for the students that have graduated from the Institution. This is a web based application that allows former students to take advantage of the benefits and services that a Institution offers after graduation. The alumni network is becoming important in the development of the institution because of their vast potential that benefits both the Institution and the students.
